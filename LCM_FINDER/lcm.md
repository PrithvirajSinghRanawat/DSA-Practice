# PROGRAM TO FIND LCM OF TWO NUMBERS.

*Given two positive integers a and b. Find the Least Common Multiple (LCM) of a and b. LCM of two numbers is the smallest number which can be divided by both numbers.*

Example:

```
Input :  a = 10, b = 5
Output :  10
Explanation : 10 is the smallest number divisible by both 10 and 5

Input :  a = 5, b = 11
Output :  55
Explanation : 55 is the smallest number divisible by both 5 and 11
